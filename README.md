# 📱 RenewWise

> **AI-powered subscription intelligence platform that helps users discover, track, and manage recurring subscriptions from one unified dashboard.**

---

## 📖 Overview

RenewWise is an AI-powered subscription intelligence platform designed to help users gain complete visibility into their recurring payments.

Instead of manually checking multiple payment apps, bank statements, and emails, RenewWise automatically detects subscriptions, tracks renewals, provides spending insights, and enables users to manage all recurring payments from a single dashboard.

The product addresses a growing consumer challenge where subscriptions are scattered across UPI, debit cards, credit cards, wallets, and digital services, making them difficult to monitor and control.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Managing subscriptions across multiple payment methods has become increasingly fragmented.

Users often:

- Forget renewal dates
- Lose track of active subscriptions
- Continue paying for services they no longer use
- Struggle to cancel subscriptions
- Overspend without realizing it
- Lack a unified view of recurring expenses

---

## 💡 Solution

RenewWise centralizes subscription management into one intelligent platform.

It automatically discovers subscriptions across multiple payment sources and provides users with complete visibility over their recurring expenses, upcoming renewals, spending patterns, and subscription health.

The platform empowers users to make informed financial decisions while reducing unnecessary spending.
